FAQ

Questions answered.

How is Prognot different from other AI clippers?
Other AI tools focus on one or two things: clipping or basic editing. Prognot runs a full 9-step AI pipeline that includes Channel DNA learning, an autonomous Director AI agent monitoring your channels 24/7, and an 8-dimension viral scoring engine. It is not a clipper. It is a content operating system.
What video formats and platforms are supported?
Prognot accepts any video format including MP4, MOV, MKV, and WebM. You can also paste YouTube links directly. Output clips are optimised for YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels, and LinkedIn Video. 4K export is included on all paid plans.
How does Channel DNA work?
When you connect a channel, Prognot analyses your existing content to extract tone, pacing, vocabulary, content themes, and audience engagement patterns. This DNA is then used to calibrate all clip selection, scoring, and Director AI recommendations specifically for your brand. The model improves with every video you process.
What is the Director AI and how often does it run?
The Director AI is an autonomous agent that runs on an hourly schedule, analysing your channel's performance data, detecting anomalies (engagement drops, viral spikes, posting pattern issues), and generating actionable recommendations. It has full context of your channel history and Channel DNA. You receive a live dashboard that is always up to date, no manual triggers needed.
How fast is the pipeline? What are the real processing times?
A typical 60-minute podcast episode produces 7–12 scored and cut clips in under 2 minutes total. The pipeline runs all 9 steps including transcription, speaker recognition, multimodal clip discovery, viral scoring, precision cutting, and cloud delivery in parallel where possible.
Is there a free plan? Do I need a credit card?
Yes. The Starter plan is free forever with 100 credits per month, enough to process a few hours of video. No credit card required. Paid plans start at $19/month and include 2,000 credits, 4K export, Channel DNA for up to 3 channels, and full Director AI access.
