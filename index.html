Channel DNA

Every channel gets its own brain.

Prognot doesn't just clip video. It learns what makes your specific audience engage. The system studies your channel, mirrors your tone, and makes every decision with your brand in mind.

Brand Voice Mirror

Point Prognot at any channel, your own or a competitor's, and it extracts the tone, pacing, vocabulary, and content patterns that define that brand. The system locks that DNA into every decision it makes going forward.

Personalised Scoring

A high score on Prognot means high-performing for your audience specifically, not a generic virality estimate. The scoring model is calibrated against your channel's actual engagement history.
